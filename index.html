Kaiting Liu

I am a PhD candidate at Leiden University in the Leiden Institute for Advanced Computer Science (LIACS), supervised by Dr. Hazel Doughty. Previously, I completed my MSc in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence at the Eindhoven University of Techonology, and my BSc in Software Engineering at South China University of Techonology.

My research interests lie in video understanding, self-supervised learning, and explainability.
